Health Risk Calculator

Welcome to the Health Risk Calculator. This tool helps you estimate your health risk based on various factors such as your age, BMI, blood pressure, and your family medical history. Once all of the information is obtained, the calculator will then provide a risk category that ranges from low risk to uninsurable.

How to Use the Calculator

  1. Click the Start button on the Calculator
  2. Enter your information
  3. Submit your responses in order to view your health risk results.
